How To

Start by choosing your league name and entering it in the "League Name" section. Then select the number of teams or entries in your league or pool. Once you have done this, decide whether this simulator will be weighted. With the weighted option, you can select the number of chances (or lottery balls) that each team/entry will get. It's similar to the NBA draft, where some teams have a higher chance of getting the number one pick overall. After you make this decision, enter the league's team names along with their weight (if you enabled this option) for a smooth simulation experience. Our custom-built algorithm is purely random and unbiased, ensuring a transparent and fair process. After filling out all fields, click Generate Order, and enjoy the results! The results page has NFL and NBA music built in to make your order reveal that much more exhilarating. Good Luck!
